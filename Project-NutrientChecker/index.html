AGRICULTURE SUSTAINABILITY
BRIEF DESCRIPTION

In today's situation, to keep pace with the population, i.e. ~1.3 billion, which will grow to ~1.7 billion people by 2050, cereal production must grow by at least 4.2% a year, more than twice the current rate.


To increase the production in the country, country needs to increse using the modern equipments. The farmers of India needs to join hands with the scientists for the same. At the current situation the country is again in the need of another green revolution, through science.
