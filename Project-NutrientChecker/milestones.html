MILESTONES

The project, that is the nutrient checker, will deliver updates on the nutrients present and needed by the soil.

So, bascically by providing all the informations that are needed for proper growth of plants we wouldn't only be able to give the plants a better nutrition but also increase the produce. Also it will help in reducing the pollution created by the overuse of fertilizers.

The cost of the product will be under anyone's budget.
